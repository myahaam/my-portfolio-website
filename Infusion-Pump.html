Project Information

  • Category: Lab Devices
  • Designed for:: Sabiotek - YTU
  • Project date: February, 2025

Project Overview

A compact, cost-effective solution for controlled liquid delivery in laboratory or medical research settings. The system allows for precise control of flow rate and volume via a microcontroller

Key Features

Custom Software

The software and the UI on the screen has multiple features and are easily controlled through a knop.

Precise extrusion

Ability to extrude with rates as low as 1 (ul/Hr).

Budget Friendly

Made with 3D printing technology and off the shelf components which make the overall cost low.
